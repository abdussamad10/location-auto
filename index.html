Radiator cooling water

Sometimes the radiator water may decrease, especially in the summer, so you should consider the water level at the pointer on the (water container) connected to the radiator, because the lack of cooling water leads to a high temperature of the motor, and this affects its performance.

engine oil

You must always follow the engine oil dipstick and make sure that the oil is at the highest point in the dipstick, because it is considered one of the main reasons for extending the life of your car engine.

Brake fluid

It is very important to check the brake fluid level before starting to drive, as any defect in the braking system may lead to catastrophic results.

Tire condition and pressure

check the general condition of the tires; check for wear; Check and adjust the tire pressure, observing the recommendations according to the load either on the door on the driver's side or in the service book.

Power steering fluid level

Your car engine must be turned off; Crouch down to tank level, this is symbolized by the handlebar logo; Use the maximum and minimum markings to determine whether to add power steering fluid.

Your triangle and your safety vest

All cars must be equipped with a safety vest and a warning triangle, in order to protect yourself and the lives of others in the face of unforeseen conditions on the road.

Car driving devices

There are three pedals that the trainee must know before starting to learn to drive; The pedal to the right is the fuel (gasoline) pedal, the one in the middle is the brake (brake), and the one on the left is the clutch pedal. in hand.

Steps before starting the car

Ensure that the sitting position is comfortable and that the movement of the driver's control of the steering wheel, transmission and pedals is smooth. Ensure the position of the interior mirror and the two side mirrors to assist while driving, as it helps in seeing other cars, the road, and pedestrians on the street.

The gearshift method for a higher speed

Depress the clutch pedal and simultaneously remove the foot from the accelerator. Then change the transmission to a higher gear (for example, from the first gear to the second gear), after changing the transmission, press the accelerator pedal and lift the foot off the clutch pedal simultaneously.

Steps to operate the vehicle

At first, release the handbrake, then press the clutch pedal firmly, put the transmission in the first position, then start the car with the key, after starting the car with the key, press the clutch pedal and the accelerator pedal, then gradually lift the foot off the clutch pedal with Keep pressing the gas pedal, then the car starts moving, you must pay attention to the speed, especially for beginners

Downshift method

Press the clutch pedal and release the accelerator pedal. If the speed is high, press the brake to reduce the speed, then change the transmission (for example, from the third gear to the second gear). If the car is running on the second gear, do not change the gear to the first gear. Had to, it's better to stop the car and restart it.

Vehicle stop and sudden stop

Press the brake pedal continuously, until the sound of the engine fades away, then press the clutch pedal and put the gear shifter in the middle and stop, and the way to stop suddenly when driving is by pressing the brake pedal quickly and then releasing it, in the event that it does not stop, change the direction of the steering wheel after brake release.
